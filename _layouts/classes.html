Spring 2014

Session Dates April 20 - May 15

Location 155 Rivington, Manhattan

The Spring 2014 session runs for two weeks on the Lower East Side of NYC at Orbital. The two week intensive will be led by SFPC co-founders Zach Lieberman and Taeyoon Choi along with SFPC alumni Jonathan Dahan and Tega Brain. Teachers will be available to support participants through individual tutoring and collective brainstorming. Additionally, classes by Gabriella Levine, Kaho Abe, Lauren McCarthy, and Kyle McDonald will provide opportunities to dive into specific topic and methods.
